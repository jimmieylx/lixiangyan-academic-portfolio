Research Areas

AI in Education

Dr. Yan investigates how AI, data analytics, and human-computer interaction can provide insightful support for learning and teaching in real settings.

Multimodal Learning Analytics

His work uses positioning, audio, physiological, and other multimodal data to reveal behavioral patterns, temporal dynamics, and interaction mechanisms in collaborative learning.

Generative AI for Learning

His research examines the opportunities, challenges, model issues, ethical considerations, and assessment implications of generative AI as a learning technology.
